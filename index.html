Register your Device using Amazon.com/code

  • Open a web browser and navigate to Amazon.com.
  • Sign in to your Amazon account.
  • Choose your device type (e.g., Fire TV, Fire Stick, Echo, etc.)
  • Click "Next" to proceed.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to register your device.
  • You may need to provide additional information, such as your device's serial number.
  • Once registered, activate your device by following the on-screen instructions.
  • You may need to pair your device with your Amazon account.

Steps to Redeem your Amazon Code

  • Open a web browser and navigate to "Amazon.com/code".
  • Sign in to your Amazon account if prompted.
  • Enter the code displayed on your device or provided by Amazon.
  • Make sure to enter the code correctly, as it is case-sensitive.
  • Choose your country or region from the drop-down menu.
  • Click "Next" to proceed.
  • Click "Redeem" to redeem your code.
  • If you're redeeming a gift card, your balance will be updated.
  • If you're activating a device, follow the on-screen instructions.
  • Pair your device and start streaming or using your device.
  • Start enjoying your exclusive benefits, such as access to premium content or device activation.
  • Explore your Amazon account to discover more features and services.
